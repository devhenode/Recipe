Jollof Spaghetti

This is an homemade Jollof Spaghetti

Jollof Spaghetti Recipe Information

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2 cookies

Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 red bell peppers, 2 large tomatoes, 1 hot pepper, 1 onion and 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsps vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 medium onion – thinly sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • I teaspoon each curry, thyme and white pepper
  • 2 maggi cubes
  • salt – to taste
  • 500g spaghetti (white or whole wheat) Auntie B (slim)😁

Instructions

  1. Place a large pan on medium heat. Heat up cooking oil. Add in sliced onions and stir-fry until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add in spices and bay leaves. Stir. Add in stew base and allow to simmer covered for 3-5 minutes. Add in maggi and salt. Taste and adjust for seasoning. Add in butter and continue to simmer on medium-low heat.
  3. While sauce is simmering – boil lots of water in a large pot. Add salt. Add pasta. As soon as pasta is wilted (about 4-5 minutes) take out 2 cups of pasta water and add into the simmering sauce.
  4. Drain the pasta of all left over liquid and add warm pasta into sauce. Stir to incorporate sauce and pasta.
  5. Cover and leave to simmer for 5-6 more minutes.
  6. Serve with Spicy Baked Chicken or Grilled Fish. Enjoy!.

Tips

For slim pasta Do not allow it spend too much time boiling, it boils faster than the Non-slim pasta. Avoid refrigerated pepper to get a refreshing and fufilling aroma
